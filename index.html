Top3

Forrest Gump

The movie is based on the novel of the same name by Winston Groom and tells the extraordinary life story of a man named Forrest Gump.Forrest Gump, portrayed by Tom Hanks, is a man with a relatively low IQ but a kind and optimistic nature. The story begins with Forrest growing up in a small town in Alabama, facing various challenges such as learning disabilities and leg braces due to a physical disability. However, through his perseverance and positive outlook, he overcomes life's obstacles to become a football star, a war hero in Vietnam, a table tennis champion, and a successful entrepreneur.typical video
